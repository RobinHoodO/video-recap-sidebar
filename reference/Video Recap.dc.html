Context Management & Token Economics

🧠
LLMs have a smart zone at conversation start and a dumb zone beyond 100k tokens where they make stupid decisions, requiring tasks sized to fit the smart zone like Martin Fowler's Refactoring and The Pragmatic Programmer principles.
📊
Grilling sessions with AI produce 25k+ tokens of valuable design concept data that should be summarized into a Product Requirements Document (PRD) detailing problem, solution, user stories, implementation, and testing decisions.
🔄
Compact summarizes session history to reduce token usage, but clearing context to reset to system prompt is preferred for maintaining consistent state across sessions.
